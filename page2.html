Minecraft

Minecraft (from the English mine - "mine; mine" + craft - "craft; create") is a sandbox indie computer game created by Swedish programmer Markus Persson and published by his studio Mojang AB. In 2009, Persson published the initial version of the game; at the end of 2011, a stable version for Windows, Linux and macOS computers was released and distributed through the official website. In the following years, Minecraft was ported to mobile devices running Android, iOS, and Windows Phone; on game consoles PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, PlayStation Vita, Xbox 360, Xbox One, New Nintendo 3DS, Nintendo Switch and Wii U; and other platforms. In 2014, Microsoft acquired the rights to Minecraft along with Mojang AB for $2.5 billion. In 2017, a multi-platform version of the game was released, combining different versions for different devices.
